# Proposal Objections — Pre-Handle Objections

Anticipates and defuses the objections a proposal will trigger. Invoked by `/proposal objections <client>`. Output: `PROPOSAL-OBJECTIONS-[Client].md`.

## Common AI-Services Objections + Responses
- **"Too expensive."** → Reframe to ROI: the cost of inaction exceeds the price; show the 3-tier option so they self-select.
- **"We already have someone / an agency."** → "Most agencies don't audit AI search visibility at all. Here's what yours is missing." Offer to complement, not replace.
- **"Does AI search even matter yet?"** → 64% of searches now involve AI; AI-referred traffic +527% YoY; share the audit gap.
- **"I need to think about it."** → Surface the real objection; offer a low-risk Starter tier as the entry.
- **"Can you guarantee results?"** → No honest agency guarantees rankings; guarantee the work, the reporting, and the audit-driven process.
- **"Why not just do it ourselves?"** → Time + expertise + the 47-factor audit system; show what they'd have to learn.

## Output
For each likely objection: the objection, a 2-3 sentence word-for-word response, and where in the proposal to pre-handle it. Tie every rebuttal to the specific client's audit findings where possible.
