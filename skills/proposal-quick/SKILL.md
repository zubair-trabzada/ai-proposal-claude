# Proposal Quick — 60-Second Proposal Outline

Fast proposal scaffold. Invoked by `/proposal quick <client>`. Do NOT launch subagents.

## Steps
1. Read any audit report in the directory, or ask for: client, service, core problem.
2. Produce a one-page outline:
   - **Hook** — one sentence on their biggest problem (use the audit score if present)
   - **Problem** — 2-3 bullets, evidenced
   - **Solution** — what you'll do, outcome-focused
   - **3-Tier Price** — Starter / Growth ★ / Dominate, one line each with price
   - **ROI one-liner** — "$X/mo to recover an estimated $Y/mo"
   - **Next step** — the single CTA
3. Keep output under 40 lines.
4. End with: "Run `/proposal build` for the full multi-agent proposal + PDF."

## Pricing reference
GEO $1.5K–8K/mo · SEO $2K–10K/mo · Reputation $1K–5K/mo · Restaurant $1.5K–6K/mo · Recruiting $3K–12K/mo. Always 3 tiers, middle is the target.
