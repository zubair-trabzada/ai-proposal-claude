# Proposal SOW — Statement of Work Generator

Generates a Statement of Work once a proposal is verbally accepted. Invoked by `/proposal sow <client>`. Output: `PROPOSAL-SOW-[Client].md`.

## Sections
1. **Parties & Effective Date** — agency, client, start date
2. **Services & Deliverables** — exact list with quantities/frequency (pull from the accepted proposal scope)
3. **Timeline & Milestones** — dates and acceptance criteria per milestone
4. **Fees & Payment Terms** — amount, schedule (e.g. 50% upfront / monthly on the 1st), late terms, what's billable
5. **Client Responsibilities** — access, approvals, assets, response times
6. **Term & Termination** — length, renewal, notice period, kill fee
7. **Exclusions & Change Orders** — out of scope items and how changes are priced
8. **Confidentiality & IP** — who owns deliverables, NDA reference
9. **Signatures** — both parties, date

## Rules
This is an operational document, not a sales pitch — be precise and unambiguous. Mirror the accepted proposal exactly. Flag with **[HAVE A LAWYER REVIEW]** that this is a template, not legal advice. Keep payment terms and termination crystal clear.
