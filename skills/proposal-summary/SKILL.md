# Proposal Summary — Executive Summary Writer

Writes the most important page of any proposal. Invoked by `/proposal summary <client>`. Output: `PROPOSAL-SUMMARY-[Client].md`.

## Structure (5 paragraphs, narrative, no bullets, ~250-350 words)
1. **Acknowledge their situation** — show you understand their business and market.
2. **State the problem/opportunity** — quantified from the audit ("you score 52/100 for AI visibility").
3. **Preview the solution** — outcome-focused, not activity-focused.
4. **Hint at outcomes** — conservative projection + a comparable result.
5. **Create urgency** — one factual reason to act now.

## Rules
Use the client's own language. Every sentence earns its place. Frame as opportunity, never failure. Many decision-makers read ONLY this page — it must stand alone.
